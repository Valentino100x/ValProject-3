The Land

Naawi-Oodena is on about 160 acres of land and has multiple low to medium density homes. We also have the Tuxedo Community Centre, Corydon Community Centre, and Manitoba Youth Centre.
This land used to be the Kapyong Barracks but after its closure in June of 2004 we fought hard to gain control of the land which lasted up until August 30, 2019 when we finally earned the land we deserved.
Our win over the canadian government showed all indigenous communities that they can and should claim the land that was promised to them hundreds of years ago.
